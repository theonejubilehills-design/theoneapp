Frequently Asked Questions

Once you have downloaded the official app, register or log in using your verified phone number. Select your desired service from the home screen, select an available date, and book a free slot in real-time. If the session has specific trainer rules (like Kickboxing), your slot will lock immediately.
To keep slot allocations optimal, any active reservation must be cancelled at least 2 hours before the start time. Cancellations inside this 2-hour window, or failing to show up, are recorded as a "No-Show." Accumulating no-show occurrences will automatically freeze your booking profile.
If a fitness session, therapy room, or reformer slot is fully booked, you can tap to join the waitlist. The system will automatically monitor the slot, and if any member cancels, a push notification is broadcasted to all waitlisted users to claim the open spot on a first-come, first-served basis.
